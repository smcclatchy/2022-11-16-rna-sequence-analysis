Who: The course is aimed at researchers who want to employ RNA sequence analysis.

Prerequisites: Some bash and R programming skills are required for successful participation. If you know basic shell commands and can manipulate R data structures (e.g. lists, matrices, data frames), you are ready for this course. Knowledge of genetics and statistics will also help you gain the most from this course. To ensure that all participants receive the support that they need during training, remote participation will not be made available. You must attend in person and on-site.
